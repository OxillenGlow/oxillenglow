📖 Quick Wiki

  • Town Defense: Protect your blue buildings, destroy all red enemy buildings.
  • Guns: All units auto-fire at nearest enemy. Damage depends on distance and height advantage.
  • Terrain: Lighter green = higher ground (damage bonus). Trees block bullets & movement.
  • Walls: Earn walls over time (faster with more houses). Click the 🧱 button, then click the map to place.
  • Rounds: Win a round → +2 stronger enemies next time. Progress saved automatically.
  • Controls: Drag to select units. Left-click to move, right-click to attack-move.
